All That Glitters Is Not Gold

The California Gold Rush is the perfect example of the creation of crazy. Crazy fools rushing in trying to find something of value so that their lives will be forever changed and finally make it to the “big time”. A world gone mad, measuring a person’s worth by material goods, by the accumulation of stuff, doling out stipends so that one can survive. A world devoted to keeping tabs on how much money and goods are streaming in, and how big our bank accounts are as we claw our way to the top of this, that, or the other; gathering, grasping, taking, pushing and shoving; building walls to contain all that we’ve gathered.
 
And all along we’ve been fooled. Fooled into thinking that all that glitters is gold, that if we make it to the top of the pyramid, or the top of our class, or anywhere other than right here as we are, we might have a chance at winning. We’ve been fooled into accumulating more and more fool’s gold and scattering it everywhere. The Good News is, it’s not too late. It is never too late. It IS Always the Right and Perfect Time.
 
We each already have the only Golden Nugget we need to live a happy, joyous and free life. The Golden Ticket to the Good Life, the only Gold Nugget truly worth its’ weight in gold, and then some. The Gold Mind we each are born with, to Be Used in Service for the Good of All. And when we fail to use what we’ve been Given, the Magical Glorious Gift of Brilliant Minds, we create matter that misses the mark, the matter that doesn’t matter.
