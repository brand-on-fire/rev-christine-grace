I’m Christine Grace

My whole life has led me to this exact moment in time in order for me to share with you

“TRUTHS THAT SET US FREE – OR NOT!”

In 2002, I was gifted this message in a lucid dream where God handed me a silver platter overflowing with everything I could ever need and more, and I was asked this question, “Christine, what will you have?” I replied, for the first time in my life without hesitation, “I’ll have it all, God, thank you!” Then God said this phrase to me, “Truths that will set you free – or not.” And this is when I laughed and said, “God, you are so funny!”, and then I awoke.

It is true. We do already have everything we could ever need and more within us, and we have been given everything as our Divine Inheritance, and now it is up to each of us to accept this Truth, or not. I believe there are many paths to Oneness and there is not a “one-size- fits-all” answer to our individual questions.
