    I’M CHRISTINE GRACE

    My whole life has led me to this exact moment in time in order for me to share with you “TRUTHS THAT SET US FREE – OR NOT!” In 2002, I was gifted this message in a lucid dream where God handed me a silver platter overflowing with everything I could ever need and more, and I was asked this question, “Christine, what will you have?” I replied, for the first time in my life without hesitation, “I’ll have it all, God, thank you!” Then God said this phrase to me, “Truths that will set you free – or not.” And this is when I laughed and said, “God, you are so funny!”, and then I awoke.

    It is true. We do already have everything we could ever need and more within us, and we have been given everything as our Divine Inheritance, and now it is up to each of us to accept this Truth, or not. I believe there are many paths to Oneness and there is not a “one-size- fits-all” answer to our individual questions. We each need to be open to knowing ourselves, loving ourselves, and trusting that we are always guided to the “Good Life” if and when we allow it, and my passion is about Being Living Proof of Living Truth, and I love to assist people in getting free and enjoying life fully, whether it is speaking from a platform to an audience, or coaching one-on- one with individuals or group.

    Truth is simple and easy, and when we allow ourselves to be who and what we are, without reservation, we live the life we have only dreamed of up till now. There are many tools, techniques, gizmos, and gadgets, theories and hypotheses, but none of them make a difference in our lives unless they are utilized, and most often, these tools we have been given, are way under-utilized.

    There is more than enough for all of us, and there is no reason that anyone need go without. What would it feel like to be the authority in your own life and prosper! Remember the phrase, “Open Sesame” in the old folk tale, “Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves?” It is a magical command that the hero uses to open a blocked cave where treasure is hidden. I invite you to try it out in your own life so that you may gain entrance to where you want and need to go. I believe we are here to play and have fun and enjoy this beautiful abundant life we have been given. At the very least, this simple idea taken from a seeming “old folk tale”, just might put you in a playful frame of mind to contemplate the possibilities you have not imagined up till now, gaining you entrance into previously inaccessible places. What do you have to lose?
