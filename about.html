Welcome

It brings me such joy to assist others in getting free from the self-imposed prisons of doubt and fear and limiting beliefs that have held us captive, struggling and striving, and continuously coming up short in some way, shape, or form. You see until we know we are the prison, the prisoner, and the warden, we will continue to blame someone or something “out there” for our perceived problems, and the story goes on and on and on, same-ole- same-ole. I absolutely love to play and have fun, and I love magic! Today, is a new day, and I would love to help you get free so that you can live a joyful life once and for all. I believe in M.A.G.I.C. and when used properly, it can free each and every one of us. You see, for me,

M.A.G.I.C. simply means Manifesting All Good In Consciousness

and whether or not you have been told “this life is all an illusion”, or not, Truth Be Told, we are here in these supposed physical forms, so let’s do this thing called life fully, and do the work to unhook from the bondage of ill-thoughts and live the life we were each meant to live. I have had several “near death experiences”, what I now call, “more life experiences”, and I have used everything that has happened to me up till now, for my Good, and I can show you how to do the same. We are here to transform our lives into abundance in all areas, and no one need accept anything less. I love to be of service and I love to assist anyone who is ready to soar. “Have wand, will travel” is my motto!

Christine Grace

Rev. Christine Grace is an expert in her field. As a Minister, Spiritual Counselor, Author, Speaker and Teacher of Truth, she combines her creative energy and intuitive gifts in service to her clients. By helping her clients see clearly where they are now, where they would like to be, and how to get there, they easily step forward with a sense of purpose and confidence. Christine has made it her life work to facilitate people in living a joyful and abundantly rich life, which are inherently ours.

You experience the qualities you are seeking in the world only to the degree that you know and live these qualities from the inside. Abundance is a an attitude and an expansive feeling; it refers more to an inner state than to worldly achievements. The worldly achievements are a by-product of this inner state. Know yourself and commit no errors in your thinking. When do you want to begin?

If what you or your organization needs is to see the truth about where you are now, where you want to be, and the way to get there, you need to talk to me. I assist you in bridging any perceived gap.

Christine holds a Master’s degree in Spiritual Psychology with an emphasis in Consciousness, Health, and Healing from the University of Santa Monica. She is a certified Life Coach through Coach for Life. Rev. Christine is an ordained Unity Minister.
