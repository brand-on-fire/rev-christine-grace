“How may I serve you?” I’m here with you and for you whether its one-on-one, creating your dream wedding, providing words of comfort for a loved one, or speaking words of inspiration. I am here to serve you!

Services

Coaching and Mentoring

These sessions are designed to support clients in getting to the Truth of the matter and maintaining momentum as they continue making changes in their lives. For so many, stepping into the Truth of who and what you are, and saying yes to your dream takes courage and vision. These sessions assist you in opening the space for the good that is already here. One-on-one private sessions are by phone or in-person.

Wedding Officiant

The sacred union of two souls requires a unique ceremony and a special set of vows. Working closely with the couple, Christine will soulfully reflect a story of love and commitment to all gathered on this magical day.

Memorial Services

A timeless tribute to commemorate a loved one passing into eternity. When you are in search of the right elegy to honor an irreplaceable life, while coming to terms with the enormous question of the unknown – I will be here to help you frame a cherished picture of the beloved and their journey in order to preserve them in our hearts for the rest of time.

Inspirational Speaker

You easily attract all good in your life when you know the Truth about who and what you are. Did you know that you experience abundance, power and respect in the world only to the degree that you feel these qualities inside? We can all draw on inner resources, but first we have to know they are there. It is all about building your consciousness and as you build your consciousness, you automatically reap the benefits of building your confidence! With an unshakable inner confidence everything is possible, from the simplest of desires to the most extravagant.
